Upload marriage and civil partnership documents

You must upload a scan or good quality photograph of your documents. If any documents are longer than one page, you must upload every page. We will only accept clear images of full pages – if you take a photograph, please ensure all 4 corners of each page are visible.

We process and protect all documents you upload in accordance with our privacy policy (opens in a new tab).

This is where you can upload copies of all required marriage documents.

If you’re currently married or in a civil partnership

You need to upload all your marriage or civil partnership certificates.

If you were married or in a civil partnership in the past

For each marriage or civil partnership, you need to upload one of the following:

  • a decree absolute or final order (opens in a new tab) to show the marriage or civil partnership has ended
  • a death certificate for your partner if their death ended the marriage or civil partnership – you can order a certificate (opens in a new tab) if their death was registered in the UK
