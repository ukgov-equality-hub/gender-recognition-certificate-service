Overseas gender recognition certificate documents

You must upload a scan or good quality photograph of your documents. If the document is longer than one page, you must upload every page. We will only accept clear images of full pages – if you take a photograph, please ensure all 4 corners of each page are visible.

We process and protect all documents you upload in accordance with our privacy policy (opens in a new tab).

If your affirmed gender is already recognised in an approved country or territory (opens in a new tab), you need a document that proves this.

The document could be:

  • something that’s equal to a Gender Recognition Certificate
  • an updated birth certificate
  • a court order
  • an entry in a legal register
